Classic

The classic Minesweeper we all know and love. To win, reveal all safe tiles without clicking on any mines. Numbered tiles indicate the number of mines hidden in the adjacent eight tiles.

Multimines

Minesweeper but with up to four mines per tile.

  • [Space]: Restart game
  • [Left Click]: Reveal tile
  • [Right Click]: Flag tile
  • [Left+Right Click]: Chord on number tile
